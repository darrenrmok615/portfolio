Based in Taipei and Pittsburgh, Darren is a visual designer, web developer and portrait photographer.

A rising Senior in the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University, he is currently pursuing his B.S. in Computer Science.

Darren was born in Calgary and has since lived in Hong Kong, Shanghai, Seoul, Lakeville, Taipei and Pittsburgh.

His hobbies include playing music (piano, drums, saxophone, erhu, a capella), collecting & reselling sneakers and investing in cryptocurrencies & NFTs.
